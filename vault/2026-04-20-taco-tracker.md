# The TACO Tracker — Trump Always Chickens Out

## Summary
A website that tracks every time Trump announces a drastic policy, threat, or ultimatum and then walks it back. "TACO" stands for "Trump Always Chickens Out." It's a political satire/accountability tracker cataloguing the pattern of bold announcements followed by reversals.

## Key Ideas
- Tracks the pattern of announce → backtrack in Trump's policy moves
- Positions itself as an accountability tool through humor
- Clever branding — the TACO acronym makes it memorable and shareable

## Why It's Interesting
- Good example of a single-concept viral site with strong branding
- Shows how a simple, repeatable pattern can be turned into a product/tracker
- Could be inspiration for similar pattern-tracking sites on other topics

## Applications
- Example of effective satirical web design
- Inspiration for building simple, viral, single-purpose tracker sites
