# Inference Engineering Print Edition Launch in India

## Summary
Philip Kiely announced that *Inference Engineering* is now available as a full-color print edition in India at Rs825, distributed through Shroff Publishers & Distributors.

The post frames the India print release as a major accessibility move, highlighting a 65% discount versus the standard price. It signals a push to broaden reach for practical AI engineering knowledge in a price-sensitive market.

## Key Ideas
- A specialized AI engineering book is now localized for Indian buyers in print.
- Pricing is positioned as the core value proposition (Rs825, 65% off standard price).
- The launch depends on regional distribution partnerships, not just global e-commerce.

## Insights & Claims
- Practical AI knowledge products are increasingly being adapted for regional markets.
